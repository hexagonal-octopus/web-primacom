A Glance of Primacom

In August 1991, PT PRIMACOM INTERBUANA (hereafter refered in as PRIMACOM) started in providing communication services concentrated in satellite communication system. As market demands an advance communication network technology with greater efficiency, PRIMACOM expands the business line by offering new and various type of services in 2006. Moreover, with high commitment to provide only the best services based on customers’ trust, PRIMACOM is growing strongly to become the most valuable Communication Solution Provider in Indonesia.
