PRIMACOM Sanitary Charity on Tanjung Sari, Bogor-2013

One of the best PRIMACOM's charity in helping people in need to have an easy access of clean water for daily living. Conducted on May 7-8, 2013 at Cibuyutan village, Tanjung Sari, Bogor, more than 20 employees were hand-in-hand to develop water reservoir and pipe over 1,5 km length.
